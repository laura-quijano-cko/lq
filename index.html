About LNO

Leverage (L) — High-impact work creating outsized results. Strategy, key decisions, unblocking teams, stakeholder alignment.


Neutral (N) — Necessary work with moderate impact. Standups, reviews, 1-1s, planning. Keep efficient.


Overhead (O) — Low-value time. Minimise, delegate, or eliminate.
